Katharine Kurtz

Aspiring Graphic Designer/Web Designer/UX Designer/Web Developer
Current Visual Arts Educator

As an arts educator, I've had the opportunity to teach studio art, photography, graphic design and web development. For any design job, I bring the breadth of knowledge and skills that I've acquired to teach these differing courses along with the ability to learn whatever additional skills I need to complete a design challenge. In order to teach students how to problem-solve, I've had to demonstrate and model those skills myself.


I've always enjoyed breaking out of my comfort zone, trying out new programs and new ways of creating art. I've been taking photographs, editing in Photoshop and designing with Illustrator for many years. More recently I've been designing and coding websites (including this one), and a few months ago I conceptualized and designed an app during the AIGA DC Shine mentorship program. I'm looking forward to delving deeper into web development as well as testing out the waters of AfterEffects and motion graphics.
