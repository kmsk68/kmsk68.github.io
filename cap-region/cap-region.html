Welcome

You've already shown that your curiosity is piqued by coming to this site and finding out that underwater hockey is real. Why not show your adventurous side by going a step further and joining us in the pool to give it a try?

All are welcome! Just bring a swim suit and a towel (and snorkeling gear if you have your own). We'll supply the equipment, instruction and the adventure.

Please email capitalregionuwh@gmail.com if you would like to attend. We'll share all the necessary details with you for practice and this will ensure we have enough equipment to accommodate everyone.
